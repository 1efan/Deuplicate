# Deuplicate

Deuplicate lets you split off copies of yourself. A duplicate is a player-like clone
that fights at your side, guards your base, and gathers resources while you are busy
with something else. You pay for every copy with your own max hearts, so each one is
a real trade-off rather than free labor.

## Quick start

1. Build up your max hearts. Craft a Heart and use it, or take hearts from other
   players by killing them.
2. Press **V** to spend hearts and create a duplicate of yourself.
3. Right-click the duplicate to open its menu and give it a job.
4. Press **C** to call your duplicates back to you, or **X** and **Z** to clear them
   and get the hearts back.

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **V** | Create a duplicate (costs max hearts) |
| **C** | Recall all of your duplicates to you |
| **X** | Clear your newest duplicate |
| **Z** | Clear all of your duplicates |

All four can be rebound in Options, Controls, under the Deuplicate category.

## What duplicates can do

- Follow and bodyguard you, fighting what you fight
- Guard a spot or defend an area on their own
- Raid and push toward enemies
- Chop trees, mine a chosen ore, harvest crops, and hunt animals, then drop the loot
  to you or into a chest you bind

See the **[full guide](GUIDE.md)** for hearts, every task, the job blocks, teams, and
the admin and server settings.
